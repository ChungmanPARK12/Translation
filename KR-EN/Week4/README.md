### English Translation 

Bloomberg reported on the 5th(local time) that Apple plans to integrate Google's artificial intelligence(AI) model into the iPhone. 

Apple has been developing its own AI system, but critics say it has fallen behind competitors in terms of performance.

The decision to adopt Google’s Gemini model for iPhones is being interpreted by the tech industry as an admission of defeat in the AI race.

- **뒤쳐지다, 부진하다(기술적)** – fallen behind
- **패배를 인정** – admission of defeat
